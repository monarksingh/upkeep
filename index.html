About Us

We, at UKHS have established ourselves as a company by providing our clients with a cost effective and value added service in the field of cleaning. Our objective is to make cleaning services essential within organizations & residentials to help create a healthy clean environment free from pollutants and bacteria as well as to create a clear understanding about the developed new methods used, changing the understanding that cleaning companies are expensive and that they are inefficient in creating a healthy clean environment.   
This is due to our highly qualified staff and skilled labor acquiring the skills of housekeeping/cleaning sanitizing/catering to ensure total customer delight, working closely together to set our unique standards and forming a strong foothold in the Pune local market. The key to our client retention has been our ability in getting curated staff to your doorstep – be it home or office. We at UKHS take pride in being not merely responsive but more proactive because we understand how important it is for our customers to have the peace of mind so that they can focus on their core business.
